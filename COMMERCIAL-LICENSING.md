- templates, products, or services that are sold, licensed, or monetized
- commercial training, workshops, or implementation services built around this

## Information To Include In A Request

Include:

- your name and organization
- whether the use is startup, internal business, client work, or hosted SaaS
- the intended product or service
- whether the use is internal only or externally distributed
- expected users or customers
- expected commercialization timeline

## Request Path

Use the repository's commercial-license issue template or contact the
maintainer directly through the repository owner's public GitHub profile.

Do not assume permission exists unless you have a separate written agreement.
